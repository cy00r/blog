About Me

I’m a blockchain systems & protocol engineer passionate about building the next generation of decentralized infrastructure. My work focuses on high-performance Rust implementations, Layer 1/Layer 2 infrastructure, and zero-knowledge rollup technologies.

I dive deep into the technical details of blockchain protocols, from consensus mechanisms to data availability solutions, and write about the engineering challenges and innovations shaping the Ethereum ecosystem and beyond.

When I’m not coding or writing, I’m exploring the latest developments in modular blockchain architectures, ZK-proof systems, and the ongoing evolution of rollup technologies.
